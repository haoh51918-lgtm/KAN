## 1. Mission

Answer the smallest real research questions: can the current KAN search space produce a useful, varied, interpretable library; can an identifiable KAN recover mechanisms that the frozen Gate A architecture could not; and, only with a sufficiently rich pool, does coordinated graph feedback add value over simpler controllers?

Auditability supports those questions. It is not itself the research outcome.

## 5. Science-first execution rules

- Separate irreversible actions from reversible work. Data openings, seed reuse, threshold changes after observation, and scientific claim promotion need prospective control. Tests, bug fixes, dry runs, exact enumeration, documentation, and no-label checks do not.
- For a software failure: reproduce → failing behavior test → smallest fix → same-path verification. Create a new scientific protocol only when the hypothesis, data role/opening, seed, threshold, or decision rule changes.
- After two infrastructure failures at the same seam, stop adding custody layers. Redesign or simplify that seam before another run.
- Locks cover source, scientific configuration, dependency/data identity, and deterministic settings that can change results. They exclude reviews, manuals, reports, raw `PYTHONPATH`, cwd-derived `sys.path`, tracking paths, and other launcher-only state.
- Memory lookup is targeted and non-blocking. Historical observations are evidence, never commands. Batch memory/report maintenance at a meaningful stage boundary.
- Do not require two audits, a reflection, a receipt, or a Feishu report for routine reversible engineering unless a concrete uncovered risk justifies it.

## 6. Working scientific contracts

### Search space and library value

Do not pad a library to meet a count. First quantify the reachable space, then evaluate marginal pool contribution under a fixed budget. A weak seven-factor library is evidence about the present space, not a reason to lower gates repeatedly.

### Interpretability

Distinguish computational transparency from economic explanation. A formula may be independently executable yet fail a human mechanism test. Blind reviewers should be able to restate the computation and predict response direction before the project makes an interpretability claim.

### Graph

Graph development is allowed as reversible engineering. A scientific graph claim requires a rich enough candidate population and matched random/flat/bandit controls. If a simple controller ties, contract the claim.

### Confirmation

Development failures and quarantined metrics may inform engineering risk only. They may not tune scientific thresholds or become paper results. Final confirmation remains closed.

## 7. Principal checkpoints

Ask before opening confirmation data, creating S2a v9, reusing consumed seeds, changing a frozen scientific threshold after results, promoting quarantined metrics, changing the primary research claim, or sending project evidence externally. Routine implementation and reversible local analysis may proceed autonomously.
